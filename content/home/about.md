I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at [Rutgers University](https://newbrunswick.rutgers.edu/), advised by Prof. [Dimitris N. Metaxas](https://people.cs.rutgers.edu/~dnm/). My research broadly lies at the intersection of large language models, retrieval-augmented generation and information retrieval. I am particularly interested in building reliable retrieval and reasoning frameworks for knowledge-intensive AI systems.

Before joining Rutgers, I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science from [Syracuse University](https://www.syracuse.edu) in 2022 and my B.Eng. degree from [Jilin University](https://jilinuniversity.cn) in 2020.
